Luke Schuyler
Web Developer | Musician | Artist

Hello all, welcome to my site. Luke Schuyler here. It's technically pronounced Luke SKY-ler, most of the time accidentally pronounced Luke SHOO-ler, but most preferably pronounced Luke Skywalker. I am a native of Nashville, TN, and received a bachelor's degree in Music Business at Belmont University. My current work is a constant pendulum swinging between Web Development and Music. As are all areas of work and study, they both carry a consistent flow of growth, and everyday I find opportunity to learn is a good day.

Being from Nashville, it's pretty standard to have music around constantly. My father was/is a songwriter and a mentor; my mother is an artist and an educator. My interests involve solving problems with programming, creating and absorbing music, and simply being with family. I am currently furthering my studies in the field of software at Nashville Software School (NSS).
